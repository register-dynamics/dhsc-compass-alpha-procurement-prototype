What this service offers

  • Centralised evidence

    Access data from the most authoritative healthcare sources in one place. This includes NICE HealthTech guidance, ODEP Accreditation, and MHRA Registration data.

  • Real-world evaluations

    Read and share evaluations from NHS trusts that are using technologies in practice. Get insights beyond formal guidance, with a focus on clinical outomes.

  • Peer contact support

    Connect directly with trusts who have evaluated technologies you're considering. Learn from their experience before making decisions.
